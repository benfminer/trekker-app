NEW FOR THE FINAL PUSH

The Campus Trail

Starting now, we’re tracking miles by campus. When you log, pick your campus — Trace North, South, East, or West. Think geographically: which direction is your school from the center of San Diego?

Your campus’s miles appear on the Campus Trail — a live page showing how far each school’s contributed miles have carried the group along the world route. Just your campus’s miles translated into a real place on the map: “Trace North has reached Texas.”
