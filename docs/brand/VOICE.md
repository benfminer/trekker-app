# Voice & Tone Guide
> Trekker App
> Last updated: 2026-03-18

## Brand Voice
- **Energetic** — This is a living, moving challenge. The language should feel alive, not static.
- **Collective** — Always "we" and "the Suns" — never individual or competitive framing.
- **Celebratory** — Milestones and progress are worth celebrating loudly.
- **Clear** — No jargon, no friction. Say the thing directly.
- **Encouraging** — Every contribution matters. The tone should reinforce that.

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Map / milestone moments | Bold, excited, high energy | "The Suns just crossed into Africa!" |
| Log entry page | Calm, clear, minimal | "Log your miles or steps." |
| Milestone celebrations | Triumphant, collective | "We made it to Europe! Keep trekking, Suns." |
| Error messages | Friendly, helpful | "Something went wrong — try that again." |
| Confirmation messages | Warm, affirming | "Nice work! Your miles have been added to the journey." |

## Words We Use
- "Log your miles" / "Log your steps"
- "The TRACE Suns" / "The Suns"
- "Keep trekking" / "Keep moving"
- "Our journey" / "Together"
- "Milestone" for route achievements
- Collective language: "we", "our", "the team"

## Words We Avoid
- Clinical or medical language of any kind
- Competitive framing: "beat", "win", "rank", "score"
- Individual focus: "your ranking", "your score"
- Technical jargon in UI copy
- "Users" — they are students, teachers, staff, or Suns

## Inspiration References
- Expedition and adventure brands — bold, collective, mission-driven language
- School spirit copy — energetic and community-focused without being childish
